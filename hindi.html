poems


i primarily use hindi and urdu as my language, while script is either in roman or devnagari. some of these are my own, while others are inspired by things i read somewhere.


set one


  • रगों में दौड़ता है अब ख़ून तुझसे ताबीर मांगता, अगर अश्क ना बने, तो फिर ये जुनूँ क्या दे?
  • The blood that now runs through my veins seeks meaning from you, If it doesn't turn into tears, then what purpose does this passion serve?


  • कुछ और भी था दिल में मलाल-ए-हिज़्र अब कुछ नहीं रहता है सिवा तेरी अदा याद। वो शाम जो तेरे साथ कभी कटती थी हँस के, अब काटती है उम्र वही तीर-ओ-क़ज़ा याद। हर नक़्श तेरे प्यार का दिल में है सजीला, मिटता नहीं वो वक़्त, न वो रौशनी याद। ख़ुशबू सी जो आई थी कभी ज़ुल्फ़ से तेरे, अब तक है वही लम्हा-ए-सुब्ह-ए-सबा याद। तेरा भी पता है न तिरी जुस्तजू बाक़ी, बस रह गया इक ज़ख़्म, वही बे-सदा याद।
  • There were other sorrows in the heart from our separation, But now, only your grace remains in my memory. Those evenings once spent laughing with you, Now feel like a lifetime, filled with pain and destiny. Every trace of your love is shining in my heart, That time does not fade, nor does that light leave me. The fragrance that once came from your flowing hair, Still lives in the memory of that morning breeze. I no longer know where you are, nor do I seek you, Only a silent wound remains, and that is what I remember.
