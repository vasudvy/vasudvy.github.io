Ilm-o-Subha

Wisdom and Dawn. These poems reflect self inquiry as Shaad navigates through his youth. All of it is being written as of now following 2025 - ?.

Kya fazl hai, thak jaana us kaam se,
jo maanga tha har roz ke iraam se.

Kya karam hai, ghut jaana us daulat mein,
jo aayi thi sajdon ke ilhaam se.

Kya niaz hai, muskil mein hona us raah pe,
jo chuni thi dil ne poore salaam se.

Kya rehma hai, chhod dena un rangon ko,
jo kabhi chune the khauf ke paimaam se.

What kind of grace is it - to tire of the very task
That I once begged for in every prayer

What kind of mercy is it - to feel suffocated by the wealth
That arrived as a revelation through my deepest prostrations?

What kind of devotion is it - to feel burdened on the very path
That the heart once embraced in full surrender and peace?

What kind of compassion is it - to abandon those colors
That were once chosen under the weight of sacred fear?

तज़्ज़िया-ए-वुजूद का मसअला है सवाल मेरा,
बस ये है कि महबूब हो कमाल मेरा।

वो तस्लीम-ए-मौत हो बस एक बयान में,
उसी इकरार पर हो तमाम मआल मेरा।

ना आरज़ू-ए-कुन्ब, ना तलब-ए-मक़ाम,
बस सिद्क़ हो निगाह में, वही जमाल मेरा।

वो डाले निगाह तो हो शनाख़्त में हैरान,
मैं हूँ उस के वक़्फ़ में इक विसाल मेरा।

वो तजस्सुम है फ़ित्रत-ए-मआसियत का,
मैं मर के बनूँ इक निक़ाल मेरा।

माँ, कितनी क़ब्रें हैं बे-नाम यहाँ,
तेरे गाँव में कैसा है ये ज़वाल मेरा।

The dissection of being is the dilemma I face,
All I seek is for my beloved to be my grace.

Let the acceptance of my death come in a single phrase,
Let my entire end rest upon that embrace.

No longing for lineage, no craving for fame,
Just truth in her eyes that alone be my name.

If she casts a glance, may it tremble with awe,
For I exist within her as a union without flaw.

She is the embodiment of innate transgression,
And I, in death, become my own rare expression.

Mother, how many graves lie here unnamed,
What sort of downfall is this of mine in your village?
ख़ुदा को हम पे है रश्क़-ए-हयात-ए-फ़ानी,
कि ज़ीस्त का ये हुनर भी इक नाज़ है आख़िर।

हर लम्हा है नज़ा की सी सरगोशियाँ लिए,
हर एक शाम में कोई अन्दाज़ है आख़िर।

जो लम्हा है, वही वारिस-ए-रौशनाई है,
न रहेगा कोई साया, ये एहतिराज़ है आख़िर।

न तजवीज़ है मंज़िल, न माज़ी का कोई बोझ,
जो अब में है, वही परवाज़ है आख़िर।

फ़ना में है सुकून-ए-ग़म, ज़ौक़-ए-बे-ख़ुदी,
जो था, न होगा फिर, यही राज़ है आख़िर।

God envies our mortal life
for this art of living is, in the end, a delicate pride.

Each moment whispers of death’s nearness; there’s a certain grace in every dusk

The present alone inherits the light - all shadows must pass, and that is the solemn truth.

There’s no promise of destination, no burden of the past — the now is our only flight.

In annihilation lies the peace of sorrow, the ecstasy of surrender — what was shall not be again, and therein lies the secret.
