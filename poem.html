Tarana-e-Khudi

Collection of poems written by me in Urdu and Hindi language, script remains Roman (I will later add Devnagri). All of it was written over a period of 2019-2022. My pen name is Shaad.

waqt ke saath khud ko tabaahiyaan kiye hue,
ab bhi hain hum naqsh-e-wafa zinda kiye hue

har shab hai dil mein gardish-e-soz-e-hijr ki,
har subah tere zikr ko sajda kiye hue

aaina-khaana hai ye wajood mera aajkal,
har aks mein tujhī ko tamaasha kiye hue

tanha hai dil magar hai basi tujhī ki sada,
khaamosh lab hain, dil ko rawaansa kiye hue

hasti se tark-e-hausla kar baithe hain hum,
jeete hain phir bhi tera hi sapna kiye hue

duniya ki har nafas ko ab dekha hai yoon,
har sannate ko dil se hum-aawaaza kiye hue

'shaad' ab bhi ho kyun goshah-nasheen,
aalam-e-tajheez-o-takfeen kiye hue.

With time, I have ruined myself,
Yet I still keep the mark of loyalty alive.

Every night, my heart turns in the pain of separation,
Every morning, I bow down to your memory.

These days, my being is like a house of mirrors,
In every reflection I only see you performing.

The heart is lonely, but your voice still lives in it,
My lips are silent, but my heart keeps moving forward.

I've given up hope in life,
Yet I still continue to live, dreaming only of you.

Now I see every breath in the world this way,
I echo every silence of the world with my heart.

Why are you still withdrawn from society, Shaad,
As if living in a state of shroud ready for funeral?

tumne dekha fakat aankhon ko,
tumne aankhon mein kahaan dekha hai?

kehte ho ki aaoge hamare khayalon mein,
humne tumhare khwabon ko kahaan dekha hai?

qaafir kehte hain ki bala-e-kaif hai wo,
kuuche mein kisi ne hamare janaze ko uth'te dekha hai?

laakh baha do tum nehrein khoon ki,
kisi ne kabhi mashooq ko chaukhta chhorte dekha hai?

ba ilzaam hai hamara badan aaj dam-ba-dam,
wali ne aaj ek Kaleem ko may-o-jam mein dekha hai.

darati hai dil ko aaj bhi dast-o-viraangi,
rind ko humne aaj liye saghar-e-jam jo dekha hai.

bahut mushkil se uthi thi awaaz o koh-e-kamar,
boltey aaj aankhon se kisi be-zubaan ko dekha hai.

You only looked into the eyes,
But did you ever see what's inside them?

You say you'll come into my thoughts,
But I've never seen myself in your dreams?

Infidels call her beautiful enough to kill anybody,
But has anyone seen my funeral pass through her street?

You may shed rivers of blood,
But has anyone seen a lover leaving her courtyard?

My entire being is of a sinner breath by breath,
The saints have seen a prophet indulging in wine today.

Loneliness still frightens the heart,
But we saw a drunkard holding the cup of jamshed today

It was so hard for a voice to rise from the pain of chest and back
I saw a speechless speaking through his eyes today.

hota hai riyaaz e bachpan
riyaaz e jawani se behad muktalif,
razaa utarti nahin ragon mein,
chamde se 'suu' guzar jaane ke baad tak.

meri khudi nakaabposh hai,
teri ayaat'on ke libaas mein
sardi failti nahin zehen mein,
jism o mashooq se gaer guzar jaane ke baad tak.

dafnaega koh-e-majan ek roz
tujhe bhi apne talak 'shaad'
makaam nahin hota biyabaan,
bazaar-o-fughan se guzar jaane ke baad tak.

The practice of childhood
Is very different from the practice of youth.

Will doesn't steep into veins,
Until it has pierced through the skin.

My sense of self is veiled,
Dressed in the verses of your signs.

Cold doesn't spread into the mind,
Until a stranger passes through the beloved's body.

One day, the mountain of longing will bury,
Even you, Shaad, in its own way.

One doesn't find solace in wilderness
Until he has passed through the markets and mourning.

fikr hai hijaab ki
hai yugul-e-shab-o-namabar hi
aae koyi ki yaadein banein
yaadein banein kaagaz tehreer e ilzaam ki.

My concern is the veil,
A knot of night and unnamed messengers of love—
Let someone come, so memories may be born
Let those memories become pages, inscribed with accusations.

ragon mein daudta hai lahu
ab tujhse tabeer mangta
agar ashq na banein
tou fir ye junuun kya de?

tasawwur ke sehra mein
tanhaa bhatakti hai sadaa
jo tuu hi na mile
tou fir ye safar kya de?

basi hain ungliyon mein
kirchiyaan kisi duaa ki ab bhi
chuyein na tere khayal ko
tou fir ye yakeen kya de?

guzarti hai sabaa
tere aariz ko chuukar
banaein agar ye sama o gulaal
tou fir gul ko karobaar kya de?

kis safai se qaatil ne
dhoyi hai ye aasteen
tere hi haanth se nikle
tou fir dil daleel kya de?

tu jurm banke baitha hai
sokh o duaa ke mayyar par
tere hi naam se hai uth'ti
tou fir ye inquilab kya de?

iss dil o veerane mein
sirf ek naam bacha hai 'shaad'
wo bhi mit gaya
tou fir khuda kya de?

Blood runs in the veins
now seeking interpretation from you
if it doesn't become tears
then what can this madness give?

In the desert of imagination
the voice wanders alone
if you yourself don't meet
then what can this journey give?

Splinters of some prayer
still reside in the fingers
if they don't touch your thoughts
then what can this faith give?

The breeze passes by,
Touching your cheek,
If that alone turns up atmosphere with full of colors,
What business then these flowers give?

With what cleverness has the killer
washed the blood-soaked cloth,
if it comes from your own hand
then what argument can the heart give?

You sit as a crime
on the scale of grief and prayer
if it rises in your very name
then what can this revolution give?

In this heart's wilderness
only one name remains, 'Shaad'
if that too is erased
then what can God give?

duniya ko dekha tou maayusiyan ghar ban gayin
har khushi ki teh mein ek be-sada dar ban gayin

jo naksh the kal talak ayaton jaise lage
wohi aaj aankhon mein gard e safar ban gayin

mashreek bhi mera magar sahil'on se door
kashtiyaan meri taa-umr hi lehar ban gayin

ab talluk ki tijaarat hai har suu zaari
dosti bhi yahan dast e safar ban gayin

'Shaad' kahun kis'sey ki kya hai
zindagi jo ek shab gazida sehar ban gayi

When I looked at the world, disappointments became home
beneath every happiness, a silent fear became the foundation

The patterns that until yesterday seemed like verses
today became dust of travel in the eyes

It is indeed my life, but far from shores
my existence became waves for a lifetime

Now there's trade of relationships everywhere
friendship too here became a helping hand for journey

'Shaad', what can I tell anyone about what it is
life that became a night-bitten dawn

dil hi tou hai tera zikr kare ya khuda keh de
har dhadkan ab tujhi se rabu-ruba keh de

har raah guzar mein tera noor dhundte hain hum
jahaan kho gaya hai tu usi ko raasta keh dein

na dekha tujhko zahir mein magar kya kam hai ye
har parde ke peeche sirf tera hi chehra keh dein

hum ne har naksh mein tujhi ko ukera hai
ab pat'thar bhi agar boley tou uska pata keh dein

'Shaad' tou mita hai roz usi ki talaash mein
jisey na paaye kabhi, waahi aasra keh dein

It's only the heart, shall it speak your name, or call you God?
Now every heart beat wants to see you face to face.

On every path, we search for your light—
Where you were lost, now we claim those streets our only path.

We've not seen you in the open, but even so—
isn't it enough behind every veil, its only your face we see.

In every shape, we carved only you—
If even a stone near me speaks, it'll speak of your address.

'Shaad' is erased daily in the search for you—
Shall we call the one we never found our only shelter?

har aah mein sadaa tera hi naam hota
jo dil na tuut'ta tou koyi paighaam hota

teri bazm tale jo ubhri thi nazar palat ke
bas wo nazzara hi mera imaan hota

bikhar gayen hain khwaab mere aanchal mein
teri palkon pe teherta tou anjaam hota

main khud se bhi gaya teri talaash mein
tu saamne hota tou kya mera ayyam hota?

Every sigh carries only your name—
Had this heart not broken, what message might it hold?

When I turned back in your gathering,
That one glance would have become my faith.

My dreams now lie scattered in my lap—
Had they rested on your lashes, what ending would've come?

I even lost myself in the search for you—
Had you stood before me,
What might my days have become?

paseene mein latpat
tere zulf ke jhaalar
sirf onthon ke peeche se
tera chehra dikhta hai.

ruuh se uthi thi jo kampan
bistar ki silvat pe jaake ruki hai
saikdon duur tera aashiyan
mere seene pe aake rukta hai.

pehle pahal tujhse
fir ye dil chehre
karwan se chunta hai
dast-e-hawas-o-fahosh mein
har ajnabee humein
marham dikhayi padta hai.

Drenched in sweat,
are the fringes of your hair,
only from behind your lips,
your face becomes visible to me.

the tremor that started from soul
halts upon the creases of the bed
your home, hundreds of miles away
comes to rest upon my chest.

First and foremost from you
then this heart begins to choose
faces from trail of women,
in the grip of lust and vulgarity
even strange women to us
appears to be soothing.
