Tarana-e-Khudi



collection of poems written primarily in urdu and hindi. some by me (they will have my initials), some by poets i like.



1. wo narm awaaz
wo ba-gila adab,
wo noorani shaksiyat.

ye sab tou pehli
baarish mein hi chali gayin.

2. rafaqaton mein pashemaniya tou hoti hain
kuch doston se bhi nadaniyan tou hoti hain

bas iss sabab se ki tujh par bahut bharosa tha
gila na bhi ho tou hairaniyaan tou hoti hain

udasiyon ka sabab kya kahein bajuz iske
ye zindagi hai... pareshaniya tou hoti hain!

3. ik tera hijr da imi hai mujhe
varna har chiiz aarzi hai mujhe

meri aankho pe do muqaddas haanth
ye andhera bhi raushni hai mujhe

ek udaasi jo itne logon me
naam lekar pukaarti hai mujhe

main sukhan me hoon uss jagah ke
jahan saans lena bhi shayari hai mujhe

4. ek zakhm chahiye mujhe
mere mijaaz ka

ye hara bhi chahiye
gehra bhi

kya he jo hogaya hun thoda bahut kharab
thoda bahut kharab tou
hona bhi chahiye.

5. chalae teer kis kis ne lekin,
mera qaatil tere siwa aur koi bhi nahin

kya batein aae the kis dil se, kis umeed se hum,
ik janaza banke uthte hain, teri mehfil se hum!

6. laga ke aag sheher ko
ye baadshah ne kaha

utha hai aaj dil mein
tamashe ka shouq bahut

jhuka ke sar sabhi shah parast bol uthey
huzoor ka shouq salamat rahey
sheher hain aur bahut!

7. kya kahein
kitne marassim the hamare ussey
wo jo ek shaks hai
muh fer ke jaane wala
main dekha hai baharo mein
chaman ko jaltey
hai koi khwaab ki
taabiir batane waala?

tum...
takkaluf ko bhi
ikhlaaz samajhte ho 'shaad'
dost hota nahin
har haanth milane wala.
